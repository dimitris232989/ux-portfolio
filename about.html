About Us

PIVAI (pronounced “pee-vai”) pioneers advancements in real-time visual AI for infrastructure safety and sustainability. Through our patented intelligent inspection and monitoring systems, we deliver a new paradigm in anomaly detection, asset management, and quantitative analysis. Our technology enables proactive decision-making, rapid resilience, and long-term reliability across critical infrastructure and environmental systems.

Our mission

Advance real-time AI technologies that make transportation safer, maintenance smarter, and our environment more sustainable.

We combine computer vision, edge AI, and robust analytics to deliver actionable insights where they are needed most - on the road, in the field, and at the edge.

Proprietary Technologies

The complete software pipeline was patented - from sensor control and data acquisition to AI inference, geolocation, and cloud integration. While leveraging best-in-class COTS sensors, we design and control the full system architecture that turns raw data into actionable intelligence.
