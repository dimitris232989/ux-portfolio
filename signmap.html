SignMap

AI-Enhanced Traffic Sign Recognition & Condition Analysis.

Home › Products › SignMap

Overview

SignMap leverages advanced computer vision, imaging sensors, and artificial intelligence to automatically identify, classify, and evaluate the condition of traffic signs. Through precise visibility and compliance analysis, SignMap enables data-driven asset inventory and maintenance management - supporting safer, more efficient, and well-maintained roadways.

Key Features

  • Automated traffic sign recognition and condition assessment
  • Damage and obstruction detection for proactive maintenance
  • Seamless integration with GIS and maintenance management systems

Solutions

Solutions for SignMap

Enable smarter and safer roadway operations through automated sign inspections and data-enabled asset and compliance management.

  • Sign inventory & condition scoring (maintenance-ready output)
  • Visibility / obstruction detection for proactive actions
  • Export-friendly data for GIS and asset platforms
